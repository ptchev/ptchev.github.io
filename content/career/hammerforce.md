**Period:** Feb 2021 – Aug 2022
**Location:** Auckland, NZ
**Organisation:** Hammerforce Group — IP licensing (pneumatic technology)

**Context:** IP licensing business scaling rapidly with two global licensing deals (>$10M each) won in 2021 with global industry leaders. One of four executive officers.

I partnered directly with engineers and technical leads to build the commercial case for their innovations — translating complex R&D into a language the board and investors could trust.

### Achievements

**Corporate Valuation Model (~$10M Capital Raise)** — Built model underpinning pricing for capital raise; based on 30+ individual licensing opportunities; externally validated by an investment bank.

**Reporting Overhaul** — Introduced monthly consolidated accrual accounts, project-based budgeting and reporting, multi-year cash forecasting; virtually eliminated manual data entry.

**Company Secretarial** — Managed board resolutions for return of capital and issuing new capital; preparation of management and board accounts; maintained share register and shareholder communications.

**Board Reporting** — Prepared and presented Finance section of monthly board reports.

**Governance** — Supported legal advisor on governance matters; risk management oversight.

**Tech Stack:** J.D. Edwards, SAP, Xero, Power BI, SQL
