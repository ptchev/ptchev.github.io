**Period:** Dec 2001 – Dec 2003
**Location:** Auckland, NZ
**Organisation:** Carter Holt Harvey — NZ's third largest listed company, largest forest owner (300,000+ hectares)

**Context:** Small team guiding corporate strategy. I partnered with foresters and harvest planners — technically demanding stakeholders who knew the land and trees inside out, but needed a financial lens to weigh short-term cash against long-term value.

### Achievements

**Forest Harvest Strategy** — Convinced management to reduce forest harvesting by approximately one-third (over 1 million tonnes per year). Built the valuation model that showed long-term value far outweighed immediate cash flow.

**Asia-Pacific Log Price Forecasting** — Established long-run log price forecasting methodology and models that underpinned forest harvest and ownership strategy.

**Divisional Strategy** — Contributed via secondment to divisional strategic review and restructure.

**Investment Assessment** — Financial assessment of investment and divestment opportunities across the forestry portfolio.
