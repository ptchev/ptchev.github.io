**Period:** Sep 1998 – Feb 2001
**Location:** London, United Kingdom
**Organisation:** Deutsche Bank — Top 10 global investment bank

**Context:** UK Custody Services provided safe-keeping, transaction processing and banking services for institutional investors with **£120 billion assets under custody**.

### Achievements

**£3M Loss Recovery** — Diagnosed and eliminated internal reporting errors that had resulted in approximately £3M annual losses in net interest income — errors that had gone undetected prior to joining.

**Financial Control Framework** — Implemented balance sheet reconciliation and financial control process providing quality assurance to senior financial control executives.

**Reporting & Forecasting** — Monthly reporting, budgeting and forecasting for UK Custody Services.
