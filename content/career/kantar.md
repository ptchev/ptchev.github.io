**Period:** May 2016 – Oct 2020
**Location:** Auckland, NZ
**Organisation:** Kantar NZ — world's largest insight, information and consultancy group (~30,000 employees)

**Context:** Member of NZ senior leadership team. Shared responsibility for sustaining and growing the business.

### Responsibilities

- **Customer Experience Domain Lead** — Accountable for revenue growth, thought leadership, internal advisory
- **Client Portfolio Leadership** — Leading relationships with key client portfolio across banking, FMCG, government
- **Advisory Best Practices Champion** — Ensuring outstanding client deliverables; developed/delivered training for consulting best practices
- **Team Management** — Managed team of Client Directors; mentor/advisor; performance and career development

### Achievements

**Pricing Strategy Advisory** — Advised Australasian bank on pricing strategy for key savings and lending products.

**Chilled Goods Brand Rationalisation** — Advised major manufacturer on brand rationalisation strategy based on product/pricing study across 4 categories.

**Voice of Customer Programme** — Won extensive international VoC programme with Kantar NZ's largest client; led multi-country implementation.

**Bank VoC Relationship** — Built relationship with major bank leading to engagement developing their VoC programme strategy; generated ongoing consultancy and research revenue streams.

**Power-Tool Start-Up Forecasting** — Built and reported comprehensive multi-country forecast model for start-up power-tool manufacturer; enabled second-round funding and successful product launch.

**Nigeria FinTech Forecasting (x2)** — Led market and product forecasting projects for two FinTech businesses in Nigeria.

**Thought Leadership** — Generated CX and AI thought leadership; presented at client events and Kantar flagship CX event (keynote). CX articles published in NZ Marketing Association and Real Estate Institute publications.

**Best Practices Training** — Developed and delivered internal consulting best practices programme across NZ and SE Asia; local champion for insight best practices.

**Team Development** — Conducted best practice audits, training, 1-on-1 coaching; increased advisory capability across the team.
