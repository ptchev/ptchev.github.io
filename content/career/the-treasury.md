**Period:** Jan 1994 – Jul 1998
**Location:** Wellington, NZ
**Organisation:** The Treasury — NZ Government's central agency for economic and financial policy

**Context:** NZ Government's central agency responsible for economic and financial policy advice and fiscal management.

### Roles

- **Vote Analyst — Health & ACC** (initial period)
- **Macro-forecasting** (later period)

### Achievements

**$400M+ Health Spending Advisory** — Advised the Minister of Finance on priorities for additional health spending within a range of initiatives totalling more than $400 million.

**$7B ACC Liability Reform** — Advised senior management to recognise ACC's unfunded claim obligations as a liability (more than $7 billion at the time), and the consequent changes to ACC's financial management needed to create a sustainable and fairer system. One of the largest fiscal reforms in NZ's accident compensation scheme history.

**Economic Forecasting** — Authored Economic Forecast chapter of the Treasury's Economic and Fiscal Update; prepared household sector forecasts used as the basis for Budget decision-making.

**Policy Advisory** — Policy advisor for Public Health issues.
